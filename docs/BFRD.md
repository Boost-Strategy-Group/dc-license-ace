# 1. Executive Summary

BOOST Learning & Credentialing Platform is an AI-powered learning, certification, credentialing, apprenticeship, and workforce development platform.

The platform enables organizations to create, deliver, track, certify, and report on learning experiences while leveraging artificial intelligence to automate much of the instructional design process.

The platform serves:

- Business BOOST! Society
- Corporate Clients
- Workforce Development Organizations
- Apprenticeship Programs
- Industry Associations
- Ambassadors
- Strategic Partners

---

# 2. Strategic Vision

Create a scalable learning ecosystem that allows Boost Strategy Group to rapidly develop and deliver high-quality educational experiences while maintaining compliance with accreditation and credentialing requirements.

The platform should allow a single course to be delivered:

- Self-Paced
- Hybrid
- Live Instructor-Led

without rebuilding content.

# 6. Learning Delivery Models

## Self-Paced
Recorded content. Learners progress independently.

## Hybrid
Recorded content plus live sessions.

## Live Instructor-Led
Live delivery only. Attendance tracked. Quizzes completed after instruction.
